  1. A New Idea

    2012

    Dan learned of the daily struggle EMS providers face when lifting patients and sewed the first Binder Lift.

  2. Early Awards

    2013

    The Nylon Binder Lift received the EMS World Innovation of the Year award and the Hot Product of the Year award from the Journal of Emergency Medicine.

  3. Market Debut

    2014

    The Vinyl Binder Lift was brought to market and received the Hot Product of the Year award from the Journal of Emergency Medicine.

  4. Safety Award

    2015

    The Binder Lift received a Safety Innovation Feature Award for helping emergency care providers avoid injuries where they happen most.

  5. Design Updates

    2019

    The Binder Lift’s buckles changed from all black to color-coded for faster and easier application. In addition, a row of handles was added to provide even more ergonomic lifting options.

  6. Brand Updates

    2023

    Binder Lift Inc rebranded as PHASE and launched new products and services to help emergency care providers move patients of any size — Safely and Easily.
